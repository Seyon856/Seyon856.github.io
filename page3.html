Steph Curry's Childhood

Steph Curry was drafted seventh overall by the Golden State Warriors in 2009 and is widely known as one of the greatest shooters in NBA history. He has changed the way basketball is played with his amazing three-point shooting. Over his career he has won four NBA championships in 2015, 2017, 2018, and 2022, earned two MVP awards including the first unanimous MVP in 2016, and been selected to many All NBA and All Star teams.
